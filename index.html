Unarguably the best polytechnic in West Africa
The Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro is an institution in Nigeria which was established by law on July 25, 1979. It has acquired great recognition and reputation since its opening. The institution is adding a new section to its education sector, the Entrepreneurship Program which is open for admission to aspiring individuals that wants to become a great entrepreneurs and acquire the knowledge of entrepreneurship.
About Us

The Federal Polytechnic, Ilaro was established by law on July 25, 1979 and opened her gates to the public on November 15, 1979. The Polytechnic has since acquired the reputation of a quinessential instition educating and training students in five schools or faculties. These are Engineering, Environmental Services, Communication and Information Technology, Management Studies and Pure and Applied Science.
